Mid-reno to move-in

From “renovation done” to “tenant-ready” — fast

Renovation projects generate layers of dust, paint overspray, adhesive residue, and construction debris that can't be managed with standard cleaning supplies. Whether you're finishing a single-unit kitchen renovation or wrapping up a multi-floor commercial buildout, TPS Pro delivers renovation cleaning services calibrated to the scope of your project. We serve property owners, landlords, property managers, and renovation contractors throughout Albany, Saratoga County, Troy, and Schenectady.

Not all renovation cleaning is the same. Mid-renovation cleanup happens between active construction phases — debris haul-out, dust control, and surface protection so the next trade can work efficiently on a safe, organized site. Post-renovation detail cleaning is the final pass after all trades are done: every surface wiped, every window polished, every fixture detailed, every floor treated to the standard that makes a property tenant-ready or guest-ready from day one. For larger commercial projects there's also the final construction clean — the most intensive version, for heavy drywall dust and residue embedded in every surface. Many clients use TPS Pro for the full sequence.

Renovation downtime costs money — every day a unit sits post-reno waiting for cleaning is a day of lost income. Our crews mobilize quickly: typically same-day or next-day for residential projects, and within 24–48 hours for larger commercial renovations. We coordinate directly with your contractor or project manager to schedule around their completion timeline.

What sets us apart: fast mobilization, direct contractor coordination, commercial-grade equipment — HEPA vacuums for fine dust capture, commercial floor scrubbers, and professional detailing tools standard cleaning services don't carry — fully insured and bonded crews with liability documentation before every job, and a room-by-room completion checklist so you know exactly what was done.

Renovation cleaning questions, answered

What's the difference between renovation cleaning and regular cleaning?

Renovation cleaning requires specialized equipment and techniques standard cleaning companies don't use. Fine construction dust is airborne and resettles for days — HEPA vacuuming and proper air circulation protocols are essential. Paint overspray, adhesive residue, grout haze, and caulk smears require solvents and tools that most residential cleaners don't carry. TPS Pro crews are trained and equipped specifically for post-renovation conditions.

Do you handle both residential and commercial renovation projects?

Yes. TPS Pro provides renovation cleaning for residential units — single-family homes, condos, apartment units — as well as commercial renovation projects including office build-outs, retail store remodels, medical facility renovations, and student housing gut-renos across the Capital Region.

Can you clean while renovation work is still ongoing?

Yes. Mid-renovation cleanup — clearing debris between phases to keep the site safe and organized — is a standard TPS Pro service. We coordinate with your contractor or project manager and can be on-site between trade phases without disrupting active construction.
