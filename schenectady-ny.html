Cleaning Services Built for Schenectady's Electric City

Schenectady is one of the Capital Region's most dynamic cities — a place where historic industrial heritage meets a rapidly modernizing downtown, growing mixed-use development, and an expanding higher education footprint. The city's nickname, the Electric City, comes from its GE legacy, and that industrial character still shapes the commercial landscape today. TPS Pro has built its Schenectady service around the city's unique mix of commercial properties, student housing, and revitalized downtown corridors.

From the Proctors Theater area on State Street to the GE facilities corridor on the east side, from SUNY Schenectady's campus housing to the new mixed-use developments reshaping downtown — TPS Pro provides commercial cleaning and janitorial services that fit the pace and scale of the Electric City. We're available 24/7, fully insured, and ready to handle everything from one-time deep cleans to long-term recurring janitorial contracts.

Downtown Schenectady — Commercial Cleaning for a Growing Market

Downtown Schenectady has undergone a significant transformation over the past decade.
