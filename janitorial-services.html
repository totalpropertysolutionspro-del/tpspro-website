What Are Janitorial Services?

Janitorial services are the recurring, scheduled cleaning tasks that keep a commercial facility functioning day to day — restrooms, floors, trash, dusting, disinfection, and common areas. Unlike a one-time deep clean, janitorial is an ongoing program tuned to your traffic and operations, typically performed daily, several times a week, weekly, or monthly.
