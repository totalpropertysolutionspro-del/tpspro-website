A clean facility isn't just presentable — it's productive. Employees work better in clean environments, clients notice, and health and safety standards are met without scrambling. TPS Pro delivers commercial janitorial services on a schedule that works for your business: daily, three times a week, weekly, or custom — with crews available for day shifts, evening, and overnight work.

Standard Janitorial Services Included

  • Vacuuming all carpeted areas, mats, and upholstered furniture
  • Hard floor sweeping, mopping, and spray buffing
  • All restrooms cleaned and sanitized — toilets, sinks, floors, mirrors, fixtures, restocked
  • Break room and kitchen cleaning — counters, sinks, microwaves, appliances, floor
  • Trash and recycling emptied and liners replaced throughout
  • Dusting of all desks, shelves, window sills, baseboards, and HVAC vents
  • Entryway and lobby cleaning — glass doors, reception surfaces, mats
  • Conference room turnovers between meetings when required
  • Disinfection of high-touch surfaces — door handles, elevator buttons, light switches
