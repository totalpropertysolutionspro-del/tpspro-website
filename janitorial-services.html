FAQ

Janitorial services questions, answered

What's the difference between janitorial and commercial cleaning?

Janitorial usually refers to the recurring, day-to-day upkeep — restrooms, trash, floors, and surfaces on a set schedule. Commercial cleaning is the broader category that also covers periodic deep cleans, floor refinishing, and specialty work. TPS Pro provides both, often under one program.

How do you keep quality consistent?

Every site has a written scope and quality checklist that crews follow on each visit, plus supervisor inspections. If anything is ever off, you have a direct line to a decision-maker who makes it right.

Can you provide service multiple times per week?

Yes. We build janitorial programs from monthly up to daily or multiple times per day, depending on your facility's traffic and needs.

Do you manage cleaning and restroom supplies?

Yes. We can supply all cleaning products and equipment and manage your restroom and breakroom consumables so you never run out.

Are you insured for commercial facilities?

Yes. TPS Pro is fully bonded and insured and provides a certificate of insurance before any work begins.
