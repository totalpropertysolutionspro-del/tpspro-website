Protect your rating

Your cleanliness score is your reputation

On Airbnb, VRBO, and every short-term rental platform, your cleanliness score is your reputation. A single bad review about a dirty bathroom or unwashed linens can derail months of 5-star momentum. TPS Pro provides professional Airbnb and short-term rental cleaning that protects your rating — consistent, thorough, and genuinely guest-ready every single turnover. Whether you manage one rental or a portfolio of vacation homes, we build a turnover schedule that syncs with your booking calendar.

We serve the Capital Region's busiest STR markets: Saratoga Springs, where racing season (July–August) drives peak demand and 2–3 hour turnover windows; Lake George and the southern Adirondack corridor through the summer season; Albany, with year-round demand from state government visitors and conference travelers; and the growing Clifton Park and Malta markets. During peak periods we run priority scheduling, crew sequencing across multiple same-day turnovers, and backup crews so a single sick-call never derails your guest experience.

Turnovers happen fast — often with only a few hours between checkout and the next check-in. We coordinate via text, email, or your preferred channel, work off your Airbnb, VRBO, or iCal booking calendar so turnovers happen automatically, and handle key handoff through lockboxes and smart lock codes. Every completed turnover gets a photo report and completion confirmation before the next guest's check-in time — and if we find damage or missing items after a guest stay, you'll hear from us before the next guest arrives, so you can file a claim within the platform's window.

A 5-star clean is a psychological experience for the guest from the moment they walk in: beds staged hotel-style, towels folded consistently, toilet paper ends folded to a point, kitchen counters cleared and sink polished, dishes run and put away, trash out of every room, and supplies restocked so the guest's first impression includes everything they need. It's these details — not just a vacuumed floor — that drive five-star reviews without asking.

FAQ

Airbnb cleaning questions, answered

How do I schedule recurring Airbnb turnovers with TPS Pro?

We set up a recurring account for you. You share your booking calendar (Airbnb, VRBO, or via iCal link) and we auto-schedule turnovers around your checkout and check-in times. For new hosts or hosts with irregular booking patterns, we can also work on demand — you notify us when a booking comes in and we schedule the turnover around it. Call (518) 948-7156 to get set up.

Can you handle same-day turnovers?

Yes. Same-day availability is a core part of TPS Pro's short-term rental service. The Capital Region's peak seasons often require same-day or 2–3 hour turnaround windows, and we staff and schedule for exactly that. Advance notice is helpful, but we handle last-minute turnovers when the schedule allows.

Do you restock supplies?

Yes — restocking from your on-site supply inventory is included in every turnover clean at no extra charge. If you want TPS Pro to purchase and deliver supplies to your property, that's available as an add-on service at cost plus a delivery fee. Most hosts keep a restocking cabinet at the property and have TPS Pro pull from it during each turnover.

What happens if you find damage after a guest checkout?

Every TPS Pro turnover includes a damage and condition report. If we find broken items, stains, or anything out of the ordinary, we photograph and document it and send you a report before the next check-in. This protects your right to file a damage claim with Airbnb within the required window.

How much does Airbnb cleaning cost in Saratoga Springs and Albany?

STR turnover cleaning is priced by property size and bedrooms. Typical pricing ranges from $65–$90 for studios and 1-bedrooms, $90–$140 for 2-bedrooms, and $140–$200+ for 3+ bedroom properties. Volume discounts are available for hosts managing multiple listings. Contact TPS Pro at (518) 948-7156 for a custom quote.

Do you cover Lake George and areas outside Albany?

Yes. TPS Pro covers the full Capital Region including Saratoga Springs, Lake George, Malta, Clifton Park, Albany, Troy, and surrounding communities. For properties in the Lake George region and Adirondack gateway areas, availability may vary during peak summer season — contact us early in the season to lock in your schedule.
