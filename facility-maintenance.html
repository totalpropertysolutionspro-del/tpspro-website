FAQ

Facility maintenance questions, answered

What types of repairs does “light handyman” cover?

Light handyman work includes bulb and fixture replacement, door hardware and hinge adjustments, minor drywall patching, caulking, weatherstripping, blind and shade replacement, cabinet hardware, basic plumbing fixture tightening (faucets, toilet seats, supply lines), and cosmetic touch-ups. We draw the line at licensed plumbing, electrical, and HVAC work — for those, we coordinate with your contractors. But the 80% of property maintenance tasks that don't require a license? We handle those under your TPS Pro contract.

How are facility maintenance contracts structured?

TPS Pro offers both monthly service agreements and on-demand property maintenance. Monthly agreements include scheduled visits, a defined scope of recurring services, and priority response for unscheduled maintenance needs. On-demand service is priced per task or per visit. Most property managers with 5+ units find a monthly contract significantly more cost-effective than on-demand pricing.

Can TPS Pro cover multiple buildings in different Albany neighborhoods?

Yes. TPS Pro operates across all of Albany and the Capital Region — we can maintain properties in multiple neighborhoods or multiple Capital Region cities under a single contract. We route crews efficiently across your portfolio based on geography and scheduling. Contact us at (518) 948-7156 to discuss your portfolio spread and build a coverage plan.

Do you handle inspection prep for housing code inspections in Albany?

Yes. TPS Pro prepares properties for Albany housing code inspections, condition documentation, and landlord-tenant walkthroughs. We clean, do minor repairs, inventory supplies, and provide a condition report so you know what an inspector will see before they arrive. We've helped Albany property managers avoid code violations by catching and fixing issues proactively before inspections.

How much does a facility maintenance contract cost?

Pricing depends on property count, total square footage, scope of services, and frequency of visits. A typical maintenance contract for a 10-unit building might range from $500–$1,500/month depending on the level of service. Contact TPS Pro at (518) 948-7156 to discuss your portfolio and receive a custom proposal.

Do you provide supply restocking for apartment building amenities?

Yes. TPS Pro restocks shared amenity supplies — laundry room soap dispensers, lobby restrooms, fitness room products, and common area cleaning supplies — on a recurring schedule as part of facility maintenance contracts. We track inventory during each visit and restock before supplies run out.
