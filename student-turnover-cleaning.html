What's Included in Every Student Turnover Clean

Student housing turnover cleaning isn't just a surface wipe-down. After a full academic year of occupancy, every unit needs thorough attention from top to bottom. Our standard student turnover package includes:

  • Full deep clean of all kitchen surfaces, appliances, cabinets interior and exterior
  • Complete bathroom sanitization — toilets, tubs, showers, tile grout, vanities
  • Carpet cleaning and spot treatment throughout all carpeted areas
  • Hard floor scrubbing, mopping, and buffing on all non-carpeted surfaces
  • Interior window cleaning, blinds dusted, window tracks cleaned
  • All walls wiped down, baseboards cleaned, light switches and door handles sanitized
  • Trash and debris removal from all rooms, closets, and common areas
  • Final walkthrough checklist completed per unit for inspection handoff

What's Included in Every Student Turnover Clean

Every TPS Pro student turnover includes a complete top-to-bottom clean of all unit areas. Property managers receive a standardized checklist for each completed unit:

  • Full kitchen deep clean — oven interior, stovetop, refrigerator, microwave, cabinets inside and out, sink and fixtures
  • All bathroom(s) scrubbed and sanitized — toilet, tub, shower, sink, mirrors, floors, fixtures
  • All bedroom(s) — floors, closets, shelving, window sills, baseboards, light switches
  • Living areas — carpets vacuumed or hard floors mopped, dusting of all surfaces
  • All trash removed from every room and trash receptacle sanitized
  • Windows cleaned interior side; window tracks and sills wiped down
  • Walls spot-cleaned; cosmetic touch-ups and minor repairs noted on unit report
  • Move-in-ready inspection report delivered to property manager upon completion
