FAQ

Commercial cleaning questions, answered

How much does commercial cleaning cost in Albany, NY?

Pricing depends on your facility's size, the scope of work, and how often you need service. Most commercial cleaning in the Albany area is priced per visit or per square foot on a recurring contract. TPS Pro provides a free on-site estimate so your quote reflects your actual space — not a generic rate.

Do you clean during or after business hours?

Both. We schedule around your operations — daytime day-porter service, evening cleans after your team leaves, or overnight service for facilities that can't have crews on the floor during the day.

Are you bonded and insured?

Yes. TPS Pro is fully bonded and insured, and we provide a certificate of insurance before any work begins — so there's zero liability exposure for your business.

Can you handle both cleaning and building repairs?

Yes — and it's our biggest advantage. TPS Pro is the only Albany cleaning company that also handles property maintenance: plumbing, drywall, painting, floor care, rooftop and HVAC upkeep. One contract, one accountable team.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Albany and the entire Capital Region, including Troy, Schenectady, Saratoga Springs, Clifton Park, Cohoes, and Colonie.
