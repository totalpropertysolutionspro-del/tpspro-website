Local knowledge

From Monument Square to the waterfront

Troy is one of the Capital Region's most dynamic cities — a place where historic architecture and active redevelopment exist side by side. The Hudson River waterfront has been transformed by new apartment construction and commercial development, downtown Troy's arts and restaurant district keeps drawing new businesses, and Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI) generates a robust student housing market with annual turnover demand every May and August.

RPI student housing turnover

Off-campus student housing near RPI turns over on the academic calendar, and property managers need a crew that understands the timeline and the inspection standards. TPS Pro provides student turnover cleaning for RPI-area housing throughout Troy's North Central neighborhood, Monument Square, and the surrounding streets — coordinating directly with landlords, working to their checklists, and delivering units ready for the next academic year on schedule.

Downtown Troy commercial & waterfront development

New restaurants, creative offices, and retail have moved into historic buildings throughout Troy's core — including the older commercial corridors on River Street. We provide recurring commercial janitorial with evening and overnight service for spaces that can't be cleaned during business hours. Along the Hudson River waterfront, we deliver post-construction cleaning for new builds and major renovations — coordinating with GCs and developers for inspection-ready handoffs on schedule.
