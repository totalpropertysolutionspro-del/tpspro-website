The one-contract advantage

One accountable team that already knows your building

TPS Pro is the only commercial cleaning company in Albany that also handles your building's maintenance. Leaky faucets, scuffed walls, failing ceiling tiles, clogged drains, rooftop and HVAC upkeep — instead of calling a separate plumber, painter, and handyman, you call one accountable team that already knows your building. For property managers and facility directors, every vendor you remove from your roster is one less invoice to reconcile, one less schedule to coordinate, and one less party pointing fingers when something goes wrong.

Deferred maintenance is the most expensive kind. A small leak becomes water damage and a mold remediation bill. A neglected rooftop curb becomes an interior flood. A worn floor becomes a liability claim. Proactive, responsive maintenance protects your asset's value, keeps tenants and employees safe, and avoids the emergency premiums that come with letting problems compound.

We maintain office buildings, retail, warehouses and industrial facilities, multi-family communities, and mixed-use commercial properties across the Capital Region. Whether you need one repair or an ongoing facility maintenance program, we scope it to your property and budget.

FAQ

Property maintenance questions, answered

Do I have to use your cleaning service to get maintenance?

No. While most clients value bundling cleaning and maintenance under one contract, we're happy to handle maintenance on its own. We'll scope whatever your property needs.

What kind of repairs can you actually handle?

Drain and plumbing work, drywall, painting, floor care, rooftop and HVAC curb upkeep, and general handyman punch-list items. For anything outside our scope, we coordinate trusted licensed trades so you still have one point of contact.

Do you offer emergency response?

Yes — 24/7. For water intrusion, urgent failures, and after-hours issues, our crews mobilize quickly across Albany and the Capital Region.

Can you set up a preventive maintenance program?

Yes. We build recurring preventive maintenance plans that catch small issues before they become expensive emergencies — ideal for property managers with multiple buildings.

Are you licensed and insured?

TPS Pro is bonded and insured, and we provide a certificate of insurance before work begins. Specialized licensed trades are engaged where the job requires it.
