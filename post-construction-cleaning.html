Post-Construction Cleaning for Albany & the Capital Region

New construction and major renovation projects generate extraordinary amounts of debris, drywall dust, adhesive residue, and surface grime. When the last subcontractor walks off the job, what remains isn't a finished space — it's a space that needs professional post-construction cleaning before any client, tenant, or building inspector sets foot inside. That's where TPS Pro comes in.

We work directly with general contractors, developers, and project managers across Albany, Saratoga Springs, Malta, Clifton Park, and Troy to coordinate post-construction cleanups that keep your project on schedule. Our crews mobilize quickly, work around punch-list timelines, and deliver spaces that are genuinely ready for handoff — not just swept and surface-wiped.

What Our Post-Construction Cleaning Covers

Every post-construction clean is scope-matched to your project. Standard services include:

  • Drywall dust removal from all surfaces — walls, ceilings, floors, sills, and fixtures
  • Construction debris collection and removal from all interior spaces
  • Interior window cleaning, frame wiping, and track cleaning
  • Floor scrubbing — hardwood, tile, concrete, and vinyl; removal of adhesive and construction residue
  • Bathroom fixture cleaning and sanitization — tubs, toilets, tile, vanities
  • Kitchen and break room surfaces, cabinets, and appliances cleaned inside and out
  • HVAC vent covers wiped and grilles dusted; light fixtures and switches cleaned
  • Final detail clean on all baseboards, door frames, and hardware
  • Trash and debris bag-out; dumpster coordination available

Serving Contractors & Developers Across the Region

TPS Pro provides post-construction cleaning services throughout the Capital Region's most active development markets:

  • Albany, NY — Downtown commercial redevelopment, office buildouts, historic renovation projects, and state government facility upgrades.
  • Saratoga Springs, NY — New construction on the outskirts of Saratoga Springs and renovation of downtown commercial properties.
  • Malta & Clifton Park, NY — Active residential and commercial development corridors in northern Saratoga County — new construction is booming here and TPS Pro is on-call for every phase.
  • Troy, NY — Urban redevelopment in the waterfront district, new apartment construction, and commercial buildouts throughout Rensselaer County.
  • Schenectady, NY — Commercial renovation projects and institutional facility upgrades.

Why GCs & Developers Work with TPS Pro

Construction timelines don't leave room for cleaning crews that show up late or do half the job. TPS Pro is built around reliability: our project managers coordinate directly with your GC or site super, we show up on the scheduled day, and we don't leave until the walkthrough passes. We carry comprehensive commercial liability insurance and are fully bonded — standard documentation available before mobilization.

For large-scale projects, we offer phased cleaning: rough clean during construction, tack clean after punch-list, and final detail clean before CO inspection. We also handle emergency post-construction cleanups for projects that need same-day or overnight turnaround.
