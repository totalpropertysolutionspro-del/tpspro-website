Built for contractors

Keep your project on schedule

New construction and major renovation projects generate extraordinary amounts of debris, drywall dust, adhesive residue, and surface grime. When the last subcontractor walks off the job, what remains isn't a finished space — it's a space that needs professional post-construction cleaning before any client, tenant, or building inspector sets foot inside. That's where TPS Pro comes in.

We work directly with general contractors, developers, and project managers across Albany, Saratoga Springs, Malta, Clifton Park, and Troy to coordinate post-construction cleanups that keep your project on schedule. Our crews mobilize quickly, work around punch-list timelines, and deliver spaces that are genuinely ready for handoff — not just swept and surface-wiped. That covers downtown Albany commercial redevelopment, office buildouts, historic renovations, new apartment construction in Troy's waterfront district, and the booming residential corridors of Malta and Clifton Park.

Construction timelines don't leave room for cleaning crews that show up late or do half the job. TPS Pro is built around reliability: our project managers coordinate directly with your GC or site super, we show up on the scheduled day, and we don't leave until the walkthrough passes. We carry comprehensive commercial liability insurance and are fully bonded — standard documentation available before mobilization.

For large-scale projects, we offer phased cleaning: rough clean during construction, tack clean after punch-list, and final detail clean before CO inspection. We also handle emergency post-construction cleanups for projects that need same-day or overnight turnaround — 24/7, 365 days a year.
