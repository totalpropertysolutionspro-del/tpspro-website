Our Story

Built to Remove Vendors From Your Plate

TPS Pro LLC was founded in 2019 on a simple frustration our clients knew all too well: keeping a commercial property in shape meant juggling a cleaning company, a plumber, a painter, and a handyman — each with their own schedule, invoice, and excuses.

We built TPS Pro to be the opposite. One company that handles both the cleaning and the hands-on maintenance, under one contract, with one point of accountability. Since then we've earned the trust of Fortune 500 facilities, apartment operators, and property managers across Albany and the Capital Region.

We're locally owned and owner-involved. When something needs attention, you reach a decision-maker — not a national franchise call center three states away.
