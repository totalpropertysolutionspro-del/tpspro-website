Our Story

Built for Property Managers Who Need Reliability

TPS Pro LLC was founded by Miguel Medina to serve the property managers and facility operators of the Albany Capital Region who needed a cleaning partner they could actually count on — one that returned calls, showed up on time, and handled the heavy lifts without excuses.

The company grew from residential cleaning into full commercial operations, quickly developing a specialty that set it apart: student housing turnover at scale. Today, TPS Pro flips 100–200+ student units every peak season (May–August) for major Albany student housing properties, including 153-unit Coda and 44-unit Mayflower.

From that foundation, TPS Pro expanded into janitorial contracts, post-construction cleaning, make-ready turnover, renovation cleanup, floor care, Airbnb cleaning, and facility maintenance — all across Albany County, Saratoga County, and the full Capital Region.
