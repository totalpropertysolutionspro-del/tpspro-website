Local knowledge

The county seat & the GlobalFoundries corridor

Ballston Spa is the seat of Saratoga County — home to county government offices, a charming village center, and a surrounding residential and commercial community that spans much of southern Saratoga County. The village has kept its historic character while growing as a business and residential hub, benefiting from its central location between Albany and Saratoga Springs along the I-87 corridor.

Village center commercial cleaning

Ballston Spa's village center commercial district includes professional offices, retail storefronts, county government facilities, and service businesses that require consistent, professional cleaning. We provide commercial janitorial contracts for village businesses — nightly or weekly service with crews trained to meet the standards client-facing businesses demand, scheduled around your hours.

GlobalFoundries corridor & the Malta area

Just south of Ballston Spa, the Malta–GlobalFoundries corridor has become one of the most significant commercial and industrial development areas in the entire Capital Region. The presence of GlobalFoundries and its supporting businesses has driven commercial real estate growth, new construction, and a sustained need for professional cleaning. We serve this corridor with commercial cleaning, post-construction cleanup, and property maintenance — and cover the surrounding communities including Malta, Clifton Park, and Mechanicville.

Residential cleaning & property maintenance

Ballston Spa's surrounding residential communities are home to a growing population of homeowners and renters. We provide make-ready cleaning for landlords and property managers across southern Saratoga County, plus ongoing property maintenance for managers overseeing multiple units in the area.
