Scope of Work

What's Included in Post-Construction Cleaning

01

Debris Removal & Rough Clean

Removal of construction debris, dust, packaging, drywall scraps, and material waste. Initial rough clean of all surfaces before detail work begins.

02

Detailed Surface Cleaning

Walls, ceilings, doors, door frames, baseboards, windowsills, light fixtures, vents, and all finish surfaces cleaned of construction dust and residue.

03

Window & Glass Cleaning

Interior window cleaning, removal of construction film, labels, and paint overspray. Glass and mirror surfaces polished to inspection standard.

04

Floor Cleaning & Protection

Hard floor cleaning and buffing. Tile grout cleaning. Carpet vacuuming and spot treatment. Protective covering removal and final floor inspection.

05

Kitchen & Bath Detail

Full clean of fixtures, appliances, cabinetry (interior and exterior), countertops, sinks, toilets, showers, and all plumbing fixtures.

06

Pre-Punch-List & Final Pass

Walk-through clean to punch-list ready standard. Final pass to ensure the space is move-in or tenant-ready for owner, GC, or client inspection.
