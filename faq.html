Pricing & getting started

Cost, insurance & scheduling

How much does commercial cleaning cost in Albany NY?

Pricing depends on the size of the space, frequency, and services needed. Most commercial janitorial contracts start around $150–$400/month for small offices. Student turnover and post-construction jobs are typically priced per unit or per square foot. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes, TPS Pro is fully insured and bonded. We carry comprehensive liability coverage and all crew members are vetted and background-checked. Certificates of insurance are available before your first service.

How quickly can you start cleaning?

For most services, we offer same-day or next-day availability. For larger contracts like student housing turnover or post-construction projects, we recommend scheduling 48–72 hours in advance to ensure full crew coverage.

Our services

Turnovers, rentals & construction cleaning

Do you do student housing turnover cleaning?

Yes — student housing turnover is one of our specialties. During peak season (May through August) we flip 800+ units in a single turn across the Capital Region including Albany, Troy, Schenectady, and Saratoga Springs — with 5,000+ units turned to date. We don't just clean: we repair, paint, patch, and turn common areas too — one experienced crew for the whole building. Learn more about student turnover cleaning.

Do you offer Airbnb and short-term rental cleaning?

Yes. We provide professional Airbnb and short-term rental turnover cleaning including linen resets, restocks, and guest-ready inspections. We serve short-term rental hosts across Albany, Saratoga Springs, and the wider Capital Region. Learn more about Airbnb cleaning services.

Can you handle post-construction cleaning?

Absolutely. Post-construction cleanup is one of our most requested services. We handle drywall dust, debris removal, window cleaning, and final polish to get your space handoff-ready. We work directly with general contractors, developers, and property managers. Learn more about post-construction cleaning.
