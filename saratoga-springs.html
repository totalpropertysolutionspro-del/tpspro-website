Local knowledge

Racing season, Skidmore & downtown

Saratoga Springs is unlike any other city in upstate New York. The Saratoga Race Course transforms the city every summer — July and August bring enormous crowds, packed hotels, overflowing short-term rental bookings, and a hospitality economy that demands near-constant cleaning and turnover service. Then there's Skidmore College, which generates a concentrated student housing market with annual May and August turnover deadlines. And underneath all of it is a growing downtown commercial market with boutique offices, restaurants, and retail that needs recurring janitorial coverage year-round.

Racing season — rapid turnovers, maximum demand

When the horses arrive in July, Saratoga Springs becomes one of the highest-demand short-term rental markets in the Northeast. Properties that spend the rest of the year lightly booked suddenly flip guests every two or three nights throughout the six-week season. We provide turnover cleaning throughout racing season — same-day scheduling, linen resets, restocking, and guest-ready presentation — coordinating directly with hosts, property managers, and co-host services, whether you have one property on Broadway or a portfolio of homes across Saratoga County.

Skidmore College student housing turnover

Skidmore College generates one of the most concentrated student housing turnover markets in Saratoga County. Every May and August we clean, sanitize, and prepare off-campus units near Skidmore on the academic calendar — turning units fast, finishing to inspection standard, and coordinating directly with leasing teams so nothing falls through the cracks.
