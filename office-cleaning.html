Why Professional Office Cleaning Matters

Clean offices aren't just about appearances. Shared keyboards, door handles, breakroom surfaces, and restrooms are how illness spreads through a workplace — and sick days are expensive. Regular, thorough cleaning and high-touch-point disinfection reduce absenteeism, improve air quality, and signal to your team that their environment is cared for. For client-facing businesses, a polished office is a silent sales tool.

What Our Office Cleaning Includes

  • Trash and recycling collection and removal
  • Dusting of desks, surfaces, fixtures, and vents
  • High-touch-point disinfection (handles, switches, shared equipment)
  • Restroom cleaning, sanitizing, and supply restocking
  • Breakroom and kitchen cleaning
  • Vacuuming carpets and mopping hard floors
  • Interior glass, partition, and entryway cleaning
  • Conference and common-area resets
  • Optional day-porter coverage for busy offices
