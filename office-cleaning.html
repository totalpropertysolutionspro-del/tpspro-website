Why it matters

Your office is the face of your business

TPS Pro keeps Albany offices clean, healthy, and presentable — without you ever having to think about it. From small professional suites to multi-floor corporate headquarters, we deliver consistent office cleaning that protects your employees' health and the impression every visitor forms the moment they walk in. Smudged glass, overflowing bins, dusty desks, and tired carpets quietly undermine the professionalism you work hard to project — our crews handle all of it on a schedule that fits your operation.

Clean offices aren't just about appearances. Shared keyboards, door handles, breakroom surfaces, and restrooms are how illness spreads through a workplace — and sick days are expensive. Regular, thorough cleaning and high-touch-point disinfection reduce absenteeism, improve air quality, and signal to your team that their environment is cared for. For client-facing businesses, a polished office is a silent sales tool.

Some offices need a visible presence during the day to keep restrooms, lobbies, and breakrooms pristine through heavy traffic — that's our day-porter service. Others prefer crews to work after the last employee leaves, so the office is fresh and untouched at open. TPS Pro does both, and we'll recommend the right mix for your facility during a free walkthrough.

FAQ

Office cleaning questions, answered

How often should an office be professionally cleaned?

Most Albany offices do best with cleaning anywhere from once a week to daily, depending on headcount, foot traffic, and whether you host clients. We'll recommend a frequency during a free walkthrough and adjust as your needs change.

Do you clean after hours so we're not disrupted?

Yes. We commonly clean in the evening after your team leaves, or overnight, so your office is fresh at open and your workday is never interrupted.

Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?

Yes. TPS Pro supplies the equipment and cleaning products. We can also manage and restock your restroom and breakroom consumables if you'd like that handled too.

Are your cleaners background-checked?

Yes. Our crews are vetted, background-checked, trained, and uniformed — the same trusted faces in your office, supervised to a written quality standard.

Can you also handle minor repairs around the office?

Absolutely — that's our advantage. TPS Pro also provides property maintenance, so plumbing, drywall, paint touch-ups, and floor care all come from one accountable team.
